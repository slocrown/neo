Frequently Asked Questions

Are EcoClean products as effective as conventional cleaners?

Yes! Our products undergo rigorous testing and are proven to clean as effectively as conventional cleaners. The difference is in the ingredients - we use plant-based power instead of harsh chemicals.

How do your refill systems work?

Purchase our starter kit with a durable, reusable bottle. When empty, order our concentrated refill pouches that use 80% less plastic. Simply pour the concentrate into your bottle and add water.

Are your products safe for children and pets?

Absolutely. All EcoClean products are formulated without harsh chemicals, synthetic fragrances, or toxic ingredients. They're safe for use around children and pets when used as directed.

Do you offer bulk purchasing for businesses?

Yes! We offer customized bulk purchasing options for businesses of all sizes. Contact our business solutions team for volume discounts and custom formulations.
