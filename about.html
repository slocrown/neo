About AquaCraft Bottles

Crafting sustainable hydration solutions with style and purpose since 2010

Our Journey

Founded in 2010, AquaCraft Bottles emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to create beautiful, functional water bottles that reduce our dependence on single-use plastics. What began as a small workshop with three passionate designers has blossomed into an industry leader known for innovation and sustainability.

Our founder, Michael Anderson, combined his expertise in industrial design with a deep commitment to environmental stewardship. He recognized that sustainable products needed to be not just eco-friendly, but also desirable - beautifully designed objects that people would cherish and use daily.

2010 Founded with focus on sustainable design
2013 Achieved carbon-neutral manufacturing
2016 Expanded to 15 international markets
2020 Launched 100% recycled material line
2024 Prevented 50M+ single-use bottles from landfills
Our Sustainable Manufacturing Facility

Our Mission

To revolutionize hydration through beautifully designed, sustainable water bottles that inspire positive environmental change. We combine premium materials with eco-conscious manufacturing to create products that people love and the planet appreciates.

Our Vision

To create a world where single-use plastic bottles are obsolete. We envision communities everywhere embracing reusable hydration as a lifestyle choice, supported by products that are as beautiful as they are functional and sustainable.
