Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products truly eco-friendly?

Yes! All EcoClean products are plant-based, biodegradable, and packaged in sustainable materials. We're certified Carbon Neutral and Plastic Negative.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Absolutely! We provide volume discounts for businesses, institutions, and organizations. Contact our sales team for custom pricing.

Can you create custom formulations?

Yes, our R&D team specializes in creating custom sustainable cleaning solutions tailored to specific needs and requirements.

What certifications do you hold?

We're B Corp Certified, EPA Safer Choice approved, and hold multiple sustainability certifications for our manufacturing processes.
